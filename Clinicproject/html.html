Cervera Clinic Website project


Process

Clinica Cervera is a Spanish Dental clinic. Our client wanted a website where their customers could easily show all the information about the clinic, specially the contact details, and contact them as well to transmit the spirit of the business.

My role in the project

I led the design and coding of the project, as well, I arrange the project and collaborated with the owner of the Clinic directly to achieve the best experience of the user and attract new customers. The website launched on May 2020.



Process:

  1. Build a confident relationships with the customer with a detailed interview. This was key to have clear why she wants to create a website and what she really wants to aim with it.
  2. Empathise with the users of the project: Used a ladding method to determinate the why, what and how of the users behind the future visits to the website, as well as, defined what are the goals of this project.
  3. Define a clear sitemap of the product and create early designs with Photoshop to present to the customer.
  4. Present different options of design.
  5. Continue interactions with the customer during the coding process to obtain feedback and rectified the project details that she did not feel comfortable with.

Solution:

The solution for this project transmit a feeling of professional, clean and tender design. I wanted to focus on a feeling of comfort and security, the same feeling that you have when someone visit the physical place of the clinic. WordPress was the platform to develop the website.
